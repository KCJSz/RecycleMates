How to use the AIChatbot

1. Click the small chat icon on the bottom right corner.

2. After that, you ask the chatbot any question you have and it will respond using 3R principles.

3. One use case of the AI can be to ask it a problem with your devices or anything in general. In this case, let's say our laptop is blue screen.

4. The AI will then give you an answer based on the 3R principles.

5. Then, based on the solution you like the most, you can ask the AI where or how to carry out the solution.

6. Finally, the AI will give you some advice and suggestions.

7. I hope that after this you know how the AIChatbot works and it helps you!
