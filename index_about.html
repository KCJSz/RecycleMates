What is RecycleMate?

Overview

RecycleMate is a project related to the 3R (reuse, repurpose, and recycle) principles that aims to reduce the amount of recyclable waste by utilizing the power of AI chatbots and the internet. RecycleMate also aims to increase the recycling rate in Malaysia and, hopefully, the world in the near future.

TOGETHER, LET'S MAKE THE WORLD A GREENER PLACE THROUGH THE POWER OF AI!

Aims and Goals

1. Exapand RecycleMate Project influcne to a global level

2. Decrease the amount of waste by as much as possible

3. Increase the awareness of 3R and it's uses

4. Show the use the many useful functions of AI and the web

5. Make a mobile app version of RecycleMate
